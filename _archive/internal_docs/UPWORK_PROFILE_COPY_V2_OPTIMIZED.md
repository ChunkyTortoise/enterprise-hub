### ‚úÖ RECOMMENDED STRATEGY:

**Starting Rate:**
```

```

**Why fixed-price wins:**
- Clients know exact cost (removes negotiation friction)

- Easier to compare vs hourly (fewer competing bids)

## üåê SECTION 7: Profile Photo (Critical for Conversion)

### IMPACT DATA:
- Profiles with photos get **40% more views**
- Professional photos get **2x more interview requests** vs casual

### PHOTO REQUIREMENTS:
‚úÖ **DO:**
- Clear face (well-lit, in focus)
- Neutral background (solid color or blurred office)
- Professional attire (button-up shirt minimum, jacket/blazer ideal)
- Direct eye contact with camera

- Square aspect ratio (400x400px minimum)

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Sunglasses, hats, or face obscured
- Group photos or selfies
- Busy backgrounds (logos, posters, clutter)
- Filters or heavy editing
- Gym/casual photos

### QUICK SOLUTION (If no professional photo):
Use **PhotoAI** or **Remini** app:
1. Upload a decent quality photo
2. Apply "professional headshot" AI enhancement
3. Download high-res version
