-   **Direct Outreach**: DM 5 CTOs/Engineering Managers asking for feedback (not a job).

**Current State**: Entry/Mid-level Developer with Enterprise Hub Portfolio
**Target State**: Senior/Specialist, $150k-$200k total comp

### 1. Immediate Actions (0-3 Months) - $10k-$30k Boost
-   **Backend/Database**: Add PostgreSQL/MongoDB, JWT Auth.
-   **DevOps**: Dockerize, Kubernetes, Terraform.
-   **Production Features**: Rate limiting, Redis caching, Background jobs (Celery).
-   **Strategic Certs**: AWS Certified Solutions Architect, Professional Scrum Master.

### 2. Medium-Term Strategies (6-12 Months) - $30k-$70k Boost
-   **Specialization**: Position as "FinTech Python Developer" or "AI Integration Specialist".
-   **Target Companies**: Stripe, Plaid, Anthropic, OpenAI.
-   **Personal Brand**: Technical blog (case studies), YouTube tutorials, Conference speaking.

### 3. Long-Term Strategies (1-3 Years) - $50k-$150k+ Boost
-   **Consulting**: Offer custom dashboards ($150-$250/hour).
-   **SaaS Product**: Spin off "Margin Hunter" or "Content Engine" as standalone products.
-   **Management**: Transition to Tech Lead or Engineering Manager.
