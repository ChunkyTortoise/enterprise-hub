## Case Studies

### Case Study #1: SaaS Pricing Strategy
**Company**: B2B SaaS (50 employees, Series A)
**Challenge**: Launching a new enterprise pricing tier, needed to model different price points
**Module Used**: Margin Hunter

**Analysis**:
The sensitivity heatmap revealed that Option B ($399/month) required 27% fewer customers to reach break-even than Option A ($299/month). Even with 20% lower conversion, they'd reach profitability faster.

**Decision**: Launched at $399/month
**Results**: Reached break-even in 6 months (450 customers)
