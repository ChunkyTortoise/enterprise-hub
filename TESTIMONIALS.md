## Case Studies (In-Depth Success Stories)

### Case Study #1: SaaS Pricing Strategy

**Company**: B2B SaaS (50 employees, Series A)
**Challenge**: Launching a new enterprise pricing tier, needed to model different price points
**Module Used**: Margin Hunter
**Timeline**: 2 hours of analysis

**Scenario**:
A SaaS company was launching an enterprise tier and debating between $299/month and $399/month. They used Margin Hunter to model both scenarios:

**Option A: $299/month**
- Variable Cost: $25/month (support, hosting)
- Fixed Costs: $150,000/month
- Break-Even: 548 customers
- Target Profit ($50k): 638 customers

**Option B: $399/month**
- Variable Cost: $25/month
- Fixed Costs: $150,000/month
- Break-Even: 401 customers
- Target Profit ($50k): 468 customers

**Analysis**:
The sensitivity heatmap revealed that Option B ($399/month) required 27% fewer customers to reach break-even. Even if they experienced 20% lower conversion due to higher price, they'd still reach profitability faster.

**Decision**: Launched at $399/month

**Results**:
- Reached break-even in 6 months (450 customers)
- Hit target profit at 9 months (500 customers)
- Validated pricing with investor pitch deck (included Margin Hunter analysis in appendix)

**Quote**:
> "Margin Hunter gave us the confidence to price higher. The data showed we didn't need to chase volume—we needed to optimize margin. That insight shaped our entire go-to-market strategy."

## FAQ for Testimonials

**Q: Can I submit anonymously?**
A: Yes! You can request anonymization (e.g., "Sarah J., SaaS Founder" instead of full name).

**Q: Can I include my company name?**
A: Yes, if you have permission. Otherwise, use generic industry labels (e.g., "B2B SaaS company").

**Q: What if I haven't achieved huge results yet?**
A: That's okay! Even small wins matter (e.g., "Saved me 30 minutes on pricing analysis").

**Q: Can I submit feedback privately?**
A: Absolutely. Mark it "Private feedback only" and I won't publish it, but I'll still read and appreciate it.

**Q: Will you edit my testimonial?**
A: Only for typos/grammar. The core message stays yours. I'll send you the edited version for approval before publishing.

**Q: What if I have constructive criticism?**
A: Please share it! Testimonials can include suggestions (e.g., "Love Margin Hunter, but I wish it had multi-product comparison").
